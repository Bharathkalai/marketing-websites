About Trae.AI

We're a team of passionate experts dedicated to transforming businesses through intelligent marketing solutions.

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Trae.AI began with a simple mission: to harness the power of artificial intelligence to revolutionize digital marketing. Our founders, a group of marketing experts and AI specialists, recognized the untapped potential of combining these two fields.

What started as a small team working out of a co-working space has grown into a full-service marketing agency with clients across multiple industries. Throughout our journey, we've remained committed to innovation, excellence, and delivering measurable results for our clients.

Today, we're proud to be at the forefront of AI-powered marketing solutions, helping businesses of all sizes achieve their goals and stay ahead in an increasingly competitive digital landscape.
